Glossary

Struggling to understand the terms at Stats Club?

We got you! Here you can find what everything means

Each league has tiers that go from A to D (A being the best and D the worst

A team's tier will depend on their power ranking value. You can find this value by clicking on the fixture button or on the overall table

Form- The average points per game, wether at home, away or overall

W by <2- The number of games that team won by 2 goals difference or more
